Your pal in mental health.



The facts are clear: mental health affects countless people around the world. Although it doesn’t always feel that way, you are undeniably and overwhelmingly not alone. Yet stigma and misinformation are still rampant and make it scary, difficult, and sometimes dangerous to reach out for help. And that’s where we come in! MindPal is your pal in mental health- providing resources for those who are struggling and factual, stigma-erasing information for those who want to learn. Take a look around.


  • For those who want to learn more about mental illness and its effect, check out our information page.

  • If you’re struggling, try our self-help features: The Void to type out your feelings, or Meditation to reach a variety of calming resources.
    Hotlines and therapist contacts are located in the top right of our navigation bar.

Thank you for visiting, and doing your part to give mental health the care and awareness it deserves.





About Us

MindPal was created by 4 girls who are passionate about fighting the mental health stigma. Using our web development coding skills, we worked hard to create in informative website by and for those with mental illnesses, as well as their loved ones. Our goal is to spread awareness, one click at a time.
